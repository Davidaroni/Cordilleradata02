Search Engine Optimization: SEO

SEO Search Engine Optimization, translated into Spanish as: “Optimization for Search Engines”. It is the technology and protocols that allow and facilitate search engines such as Google or Bing, provide the best solution or information to the person or user who is looking for a product or service; with the information and data provided by the web pages or social media pages.
When a person searches for a product, service or information, in search engines using keywords or voice. You are presented with a list of alternatives ordered according to the criteria that best solve your need, That is to say, it is about presenting exactly what you were looking for.
To offer exactly what the person is looking for; SEO protocols and standards are used in the process of: Identification through characteristics and properties, elaboration of semantic content, categorization of structured data. Also considering criteria such as simplification for easy understanding, lightness of data. And to occupy the first places on the lists, the criterion of differentiation is used based on market segmentation.
The main focus of SEO is to improve visibility and organic traffic in the long term. This is achieved through optimization of on-page elements such as keywords, HTML tags, quality content, site structure, loading speed and other factors that search engines consider relevant.

The SEO process is implemented together with web design

Analysis

Analysis of the product or service with a focus on marketing. To evaluate the product or service, its characteristics, its presentation, its uses and characteristics.

Evaluation

Evaluate and analyze the market, to determine and quantify the market segment; The market segment is the consumer public who also has the decision to buy.

Preparation

Preparation of text content that defines products and services, as well as their nature, uses, advantages, characteristics, versions, models, offers and campaigns, accompanied by catalogues, images, references, etc.

Keywords

Creation of metadata with key words, and description of the web page, with labeling.

Structured data

Creation of structured data with the https://schema.org protocol.

SEO measurement

Evaluation and measurement of the SEO of the website, and its readjustment.

Importance of SEO

SEO is important because it allows you to have a sales channel from the internet. There are many products and services whose sales are generated by Google, because people They search for products and services and order the purchase via telephone, email or social networks.
It is also a channel through which quotes for services or invitations to participate in private or public tenders for construction projects, supply of products, temporary services or social projects.

What is Organic SEO

Organic SEO are the techniques and strategies aimed at ensuring that a web page provides the data and the precise information that a client requires when searching on the Internet. And since there is competition, It is necessary to position yourself at the top of the search list, based on the precision of the data, differentiation, speed, usefulness and usability to demonstrate to search engines the importance of page by providing the solution and or product that the consumer is looking for.
