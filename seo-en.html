Search Engine Optimization: SEO

SEO, Search Engine Optimization, is the technology and protocols that enable and facilitate search engines like Google or Bing to provide the best solution or information to the person or user searching for a product or service, using the information and data provided by web pages or social media pages.
When a person searches for a product, service, or information on search engines using keywords or voice, they are presented with a list of alternatives arranged according to the criterion that can best address their needs. In other words, the goal is to present exactly what they were looking for.
To provide precisely what the person is seeking, SEO protocols and standards are used in the process of identifying through characteristics and properties, creating semantic content, and categorizing structured data. Criteria like simplification for easy understanding and data efficiency are also considered. To rank at the top of the lists, the criterion of differentiation based on market segmentation is applied.
The primary focus of SEO is to improve long-term visibility and organic traffic. This is achieved through the optimization of on-page elements, such as keywords, HTML tags, high-quality content, site structure, loading speed, and other factors considered relevant by search engines.

The SEO process is implemented in conjunction with web design

Analysis

Analysis of the product or service with a marketing focus. This evaluation includes the product or service, its features, its presentation, its uses, and its characteristics.

Evaluation

Evaluate and analyze the market, to determine and quantify the market segment; The market segment is the consumer public who also has the decision to buy.

Preparation

Preparation of text content that defines products and services, as well as their nature, uses, advantages, characteristics, versions, models, offers and campaigns, accompanied by catalogues, images, references, etc.

Keywords

Creation of metadata with key words, and description of the web page, with labeling.

Structured data

Creation of structured data with the https://schema.org protocol.

SEO measurement

Evaluation and measurement of the SEO of the website, and its readjustment.

Importance of SEO

SEO is considered important because a sales channel from the internet can be provided. Many products and services are generated by Google, as products and services are searched for by people, and orders are placed via telephone, email, or social networks.
It is also a channel through which service quotes are requested or invitations to participate in private or public tenders for construction projects, product supply, temporary services, or social projects.

What is Organic SEO

Organic or natural SEO refers to techniques and strategies aimed at ensuring that a web page provides the accurate data and information a customer requires when conducting an internet search. Given the competition, it is essential to secure a position at the top of the search results list, based on data accuracy, differentiation, speed, utility, and usability, to demonstrate to search engines the significance of the page in delivering the solution or product the consumer is seeking.
