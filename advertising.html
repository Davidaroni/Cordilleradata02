Advertising in internet

Advertising is the dissemination of advertisements, messages, and news to a specific audience ('market segment') in order to make known the existence, characteristics, details, qualities, benefits, opinions, references, and feelings of certain products, services, and events. With the aim of influencing behavior, typically persuading them to take a desired action, such as purchasing a product, hiring a service, attending an event, or adopting a specific opinion.

Advertising with websites

The website is an advertising channel for a company, as it contains the basic information that identifies it through the history of the company's formation, organizational structure, mission, the products, services, and solutions it offers, as well as the brands it owns. It also provides detailed information about products and services through catalogs and online stores. Additionally, it offers the means to make purchases and contacts for requesting quotes.
The business website serves as the link and final destination for social media pages because it is where customers make purchases or visit to verify the company's information and reliability.
The website is equipped with tools such as Google Analytics, which allows measuring visit parameters, the source of traffic, user behavior, the keywords visitors are searching for, and the number of people filling out forms or requesting information, as well as demographic and geographic data.

Creation of Google My Business

Google My Business is a tool created by Google to manage a company's online presence. It allows entering the company's name, its address, and contact information, which are indexed on a geographical map. It also enables inputting the industry, sector, and the services and products offered, as well as details such as opening hours and public ratings.
