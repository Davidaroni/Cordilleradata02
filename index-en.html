Advertising Services Internet marketing

The company: Cordillera data

It is a Peruvian company specialized in providing advertising services, market research, and opinion analysis. We also handle project development and evaluation. Our advertising services are based on operational marketing, which involves evaluating the product or service, identifying the target market segment, and selecting advertising channels. We offer the following channels: designing SEO-optimized websites, social media, audio or podcast, and print advertising.
Market research is conducted to determine and identify both product and service providers and demand. It quantifies demand and identifies relevant market characteristics. Opinion analysis is conducted to gauge the general public's opinions regarding political and social aspects, as well as the prestige of private and public brands and institutions.
Project development and evaluation involve creating a document that contains all the necessary information to demonstrate the viability or inviability of a project, along with the information required to initiate and operate the project.
